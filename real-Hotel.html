凯悦嘉轩酒店项目 Hyatt Place at Ballantyne Village

地理位置：美国，北卡罗来纳州，夏洛特市，巴伦坦区，巴伦坦休闲购物中心

Location: United States, Charlotte, Ballantyne Area, Ballantyne Village

 

项目说明： “凯悦嘉轩”品牌(计划使用)酒店，160套客房

Project Description: 160-unit Hyatt Place (projected) hotel rooms

 

项目亮点：

Project Highlights:

  • 卓越的地理位置：根据美国最大房地产搜索网站之一 Realtor.com的统计，在2013年巴伦坦区是全美搜索率排名第五的繁华城区，也是美国南部十大发展最快区域之一；该区毗邻I-485号高速公路，20分钟左右车程便可到达夏洛特道格拉斯国际机场以及夏洛特市中心。

    Prime location: Ballantyne was ranked as the Top 5 most searched areas according to one of the largest real estate search websites in the U.S. (www.realtor.com) in 2013. Ballantyne Area is also TOP 10 fastest development area in the Southern US. It is close to I-485, and there is a 20-minute drive from Ballantyne to uptown Charlotte and Charlotte Douglas International Airport.

  • 毗邻巴伦坦区休闲购物中心：作为巴伦坦区最大的购物中心之一，巴伦坦休闲购物中心每年吸引大量人流来往。酒店选址在购物中心旁，届时酒店租客步行便可极为便利的购物和休闲。

    Proximity to Ballantyne Village: As one of the busiest shopping centers in the area, Ballantyne Village attracts a large population every year. The hotel guests can easily walk to Ballantyne Village for shopping and recreation.

  • 优异的酒店品牌：本项目计划使用“凯悦嘉轩”品牌打造酒店。凯悦嘉轩酒店是酒店业知名品牌，客房空间宽敞，拥有一流配套设施，入住宾客满意度在同级别中高档酒店中排名第一 。

    Well-known hotel brand: The project plans to adopt the well-known brand in the hotel industry, Hyatt Place, which features spacious guest room and first-class facilities and ranks first at guest satisfaction among mid-scale hotels.

  • 可观的租金定位：巴伦坦属于夏洛特市重点建设的高端区域，租金定位将高于平均水平。

    Stable source of income: Ballantyne is one of the high-end areas in Charlotte. Thus the hotel room rate will be above average.

  • 入住率有保障：在巴伦坦区现存酒店远不能满足市场所需，供不应求使得酒店建成后的入住率有所保障。

    Low vacancy rate: The current hotel rooms in the area can not meet the market demand, thus the hotel will have reasonable high occupancy.
