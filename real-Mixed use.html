混合用途项目 Mixed use

混合用途项目Mixed use University City Station Mixed-used Development Project

地理位置：美国，北卡罗来纳州，夏洛特市，大学城区

Project Location: United States, North Carolina, Charlotte, University City Area

 

项目说明：现代化学生公寓及特色零售街综合性地产项目

Project Description: Modern Student Apartments and Retail Space Development

 

配套设施：度假风泳池、SPA池、俱乐部休闲会所、公共休闲区、健身房以及停车场等

Amenities: Resort Style Swimming Pool, SPA Pool, Clubhouse, Public Recreation Area, Gym and Parking Lots.

 

项目亮点：

Project Highlights:

  • 卓越的地理位置：夏洛特市轻轨延伸线路连接夏洛特市中心和北卡大学夏洛特分校校园，项目位于此轻轨线路旁，紧邻大学城站。项目还临近夏洛特市几条主要高速路口。项目接近多个购物区以及大型商超，如宜家、沃尔玛等；毗邻医院和休闲餐饮区。大学城城区是夏洛特市雇员最多的地区之一，有超过73,000名雇员，北卡大学夏洛特分校目前有27,200名学生。

    Prime location: The project is located at the University City station along the light rail extension line connecting the university campus with uptown Charlotte, with convenient access to major highways. It is also within close proximity to education, office, retail (e.g. Ikea, Walmart), dining, entertainment and medical facility in the University City area. University City is one of the largest employment centers in Charlotte with 73,000 employees.

  • 强烈市场需求：北卡大学夏洛特分校计划于2020年由27,200名学生增加到35,000名学生，从而极大增加市场上的学生公寓需求量。

    Strong demand in the market: UNC-Charlotte will increase the enrollment from 27,200 to 35,000 by 2020, which brings strong demand of student housing in this market.

  • 无直接竞争：学生可乘坐轻轨直接到达大学校园，目前市场上尚无同类产品有此便利条件; 该项目步行范围内便可到达项目的零售物业区域，届时项目的租户可惠及零售物业主，而零售商铺所带来的便利也可吸引到更多租户，实现双赢。

    No direct competition within the market: The project has a unique strategy to develop along the light rail. The light rail will take students directly to the university campus. None of the current off-campus student housing projects in the area has such convenient access to the campus. Meanwhile, the project creates a win-win situation for both renters and retail owners as renters will bring businesses to retail shops and convenient shopping will attract renters.

  • 可持续性参与市场竞争：项目在设计时已做长期考量，以高质量节能标准来设计项目。

    Sustainable design: The project adopts energy-saving urban style design to set a high standard for sustainable competitive advantage.

  • 一流的开发团队：项目方坚持与市场上业绩最为出色的服务商合作，如：业主代表选用仲量联行(JLL)，物业公司选用Greystar。

    First-rate development team: The project teams up with the first-rate service providers with Jones Lang LaSalle as the Project Management team and Greystar as the Property Management team.
