Operations Analyst (ID: OA-010)

  • 1. Use organizational research tools to monitor the external environment, both proactively and upon request, and identify relevant dynamics with potential to impact project management processes.
  • 2. Develop sophisticated data analyses, inclusive of data preparation, data processing, quantitative data analysis, and advanced visualization techniques, such as using excel pivot tables and what-if analysis functions to run sensitivity tests for real estate projects.
  • 3. Interface with customers in consulting/expert capacity to translate business needs into reporting and analysis deliverables.
  • 4. Employ cutting-edge technology to mine customer interactions, through recorded conversations and social media activity, and incorporates findings into analysis deliverables, such as monitoring social media marketing campaigns for apartment assets and participate tenant events to give feedbacks to the onsite apartment property management team.
  • 5. Drive organizational data integrity by employing data governance frameworks and consulting on defining data elements and data governance processes.
  • 6. Support management in the production of strategic communications and documents through the effective use of visuals and clear, cohesive messaging, such as project progress reports, company pitches, product and service brochures, in the form of PPT, Videos, PDF.
  • 7. Conduct surveys and collect information on operational and administrative problems; develop recommendations for problem resolution; coordinate and participate in special research projects, such as conducting apartment tours, identifying property operational problems and providing recommendations on possible solutions.
  • 8. Compile and prepare reports, memoranda, policies, manuals and newsletters, such as project progress reports, project offering memorandums, newsletters to investors.
  • 9. Prepare tables, charts and graphs to illustrate distribution and trends of statistical and financial data, utilizing financial modeling skills.
  • 10. Participate in the development and installation of new or revised programs, systems, procedures and methods of operation.
  • 11. Work with the real estate development team to analyze market data and conduct predevelopment activities to help make real-time business decisions.
  • 12. Provide bilingual service to international clients, including but not limited to interpreting and materials translating to facilitate the communication between oversea investors and project development team.
  • 13. Follows the real estate market trends as well as EB-5 industry policies, and presents the updates to the clients on a regular basis.
