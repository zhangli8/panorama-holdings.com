公寓类项目 Apartment

北卡大学综合园公寓项目(UCC)University City Community Apartments Project

地理位置：美国，北卡罗来纳州，夏洛特市，大学城区

Project Location: University City area, Charlotte, North Carolina, United States

 

项目说明：370套多户式精品公寓

Project Description: 370-unit Class-A Multifamily Apartments

www.bluatnorthline.com

 

设计风格：城市风格

Design: Urban Style

 

配套设施：度假风泳池、SPA池、俱乐部休闲会所、公共休闲区、健身房以及停车场等

Amenities: Resort Style Swimming Pool, SPA Pool, Clubhouse, Public Recreation Area, Gym and Parking Lots.

 

项目亮点：

Project Highlights:

  • 卓越的地理位置：夏洛特市轻轨延伸线路连接夏洛特市中心和北卡大学夏洛特分校校园，项目位于此轻轨线路旁，并可步行至两个重要轻轨站。项目还临近夏洛特市几条主要高速路口。项目接近多个购物区以及大型商超，如宜家、沃尔玛等；毗邻医院和休闲餐饮区。大学城城区是夏洛特市雇员最多的地区之一，有超过73,000名雇员，北卡大学夏洛特分校目前有28,000名学生。

    Prime location: The project is located along the light rail extension line connecting the university campus with uptown Charlotte, within walking distance to two stations and with convenient access to major highways. It is also within close proximity to education, office, retail (e.g. Ikea, Walmart), dining, entertainment and medical facility in the University City area. University City is one of the largest employment centers in Charlotte with 73,000 employees and home to the campus of UNC-Charlotte with 28,000 enrollments.

  • 市场覆盖范围广：位于大学城轻轨站（步行仅三分钟），轻轨扩建工程于2018年3月16日正式通车项目租户乘坐轻轨可到达北卡大学校园以及市中心，届时项目将吸引到北卡大学的学生和教员、大学研究园的职员以及在夏洛特市中心供职的职员。

    Wide-range of target market: The light rail extension line will bring potential renters from students and faculty members at UNC-Charlotte as well as employees from University Research Park and uptown Charlotte.

  • 租金有保障：大学城城区人口主要由在读学生、毕业学生、教员以及公司职员组成，选择租房的人口大于持有房产的人口；该区就业率高、发展快、发展空间大 (IBM、TIAA-CREF、Wells Fargo等大型企业中后台位于大学研究园)。

    Stable source of rental income: University City has more renters than property owners as the population is composed of students, graduates, faculty members, and employees.

  • 无直接竞争：市场内尚无同类产品在轻轨站沿线进行开发；项目设计理念(轻轨站公寓概念)和模式很难被复制。

    No direct competition within the market: The project has a unique strategy to develop along the light rail, which is a rare product in the multifamily development market.

  • 可持续性：项目在设计时已做长期考量，以高质量现代城市风格的标准来设计公寓，楼内设有电梯、每套公寓均配有家电，完美地将绿色节能和人性化设计融合在项目中。

    Sustainable design: The project adopts energy-saving urban style design to set a high standard for sustainable competitive advantage.

  • 一流的团队：项目方坚持与市场上业绩最为出色的服务商合作，如：业主代表选用仲量联行(JLL)，物业公司选用Greystar。

    First-rate team: Jones Lang LaSalle is the Project Management team and Greystar is the Property Management team.
