Real Estate Development 房地产开发

磐远地产开发有限公司是磐远控股下属专业房地产开发公司。其竞争优势在于结合宏观层面美国房地产市场分析与微观层面房地产开发实践，从而准确定位项目并成功执行项目。所有开发项目的投资策略和预计回报均经过多方论证。在我们一流团队的全力支持下，我们以专业和精益求精的态度对待开发过程中的每一步骤，既掌控全局观又注重细节。

磐远地产开发有限公司目前项目总计超过一百万平方英尺，资产类别涵盖传统多户公寓开发，学生公寓，零售物业，高档 全方位服务酒店，以及中高档有限服务酒店。

Panorama Development operates one of the core sections at Panorama Holdings: real estate development. Its competitive advantage is the combination of macro-level analysis of the real estate market and micro-level operation of development projects in the United States to accurately source deals and successfully execute the programs. The development projects are well tested in terms of investment strategies and projection of returns. With the full support of our first-rate service teams, we take each step of the development process with professionalism and perfectionism, both embracing comprehensive views and zeroing in on details.

Panorama Development currently has a project pipeline that totals over 1 million square feet, with asset classes spanning over conventional multifamily development, student housing, retail space, 4-star full-service hotel, and 3-star limited-service hotel.

仲量联行
