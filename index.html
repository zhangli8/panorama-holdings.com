Panorama Holdings 磐远控股 详细more

磐远控股主营业务包括房地产开发，投资，金融，咨询，及投资移民。磐远控股旗下拥有专业房地产开发公司，其竞争优势在于结合宏观层面美国房地产市场分析与微观层面房地产开发实践，从而准确定位项目并成功执行项目。所有开发项目的投资策略和预计回报均经过多方论证。在我们一流团队的全力支持下，我们以专业和精益求精的态度对待开发过程中的每一步骤，既掌控全局观又注重细节。

磐远控股目前在夏洛特地区项目总计超过三百万平方英尺，资产类别涵盖传统多户公寓开发，办公楼，以及酒店。

磐远控股下属多家美国移民局认证的区域中心，专业从事美国移民法规定的各种投资移民项目开发，融资，和管理, 以卓越的服务为外籍EB-5投资人取得美国永居身份（绿卡）和降低投资风险。

Panorama Holdings specializes in real estate development, investment, finance, consultation, and EB-5 projects. The real estate development section combines macro-level analysis of the real estate market and micro-level operation of development projects in the United States to accurately source deals and successfully execute the programs. The development projects are well tested in terms of investment strategies and projection of returns. With the full support of our first-rate development teams, we take each step of the development process with professionalism and perfectionism, both embracing comprehensive views and zeroing in on details.

Panorama Holdings currently has a project pipeline that totals over 3 million square feet in Charlotte, with asset classes spanning over conventional multifamily development, office space, and hotels.

Panorama Holdings owns multiple USCIS-designated regional centers to develop, finance and manage investment projects under the EB-5 program, facilitating investors to obtain permanent residency, increase return of investment and lower risk of investment.
